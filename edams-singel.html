EDAMS

Project Date Oct 25th, 2023
Role Android
Client QS Logics
Visit -

The Employee Daliy Management System (EDAMS) is an in-house application designed with role-based access control. Users, based on their roles, are required to check in within assigned locations. Each employee may have multiple assigned sites, each with one or more assigned locations. For a check-in action to be enabled, the user's current location must be within a 30-meter radius around the assigned department. If the user's current location does not meet this requirement, the check-in button will not be activated. However, there are exceptional cases where users can still check in with their current location outside of the assigned department. In such exceptional cases, users will be prompted with an alert dialog requesting them to provide a reason for checking in outside of the assigned department. This additional step ensures accountability and allows users to justify their actions. All check-in and check-out details, including instances where employees check in outside of the assigned department, are recorded and listed in the admin panels. Admins have access to review these details and can investigate why an employee checked in outside of their assigned department. This feature provides transparency and oversight, allowing administrators to ensure compliance with company policies and procedures.

In EDAMS (Employee Daliy Management System), the application's design and functionality adapt to accommodate multiple organizations, each with its own unique theme, profile, and color scheme. This customization ensures that the user interface reflects the branding and identity of each organization, providing a cohesive and personalized user experience. EDAMS implements RBAC to manage user permissions and access levels within each organization. Employees can submit leave requests and mass record submissions through the system. The submission process follows Hierarchy-Based Approval Workflow: based on the user's role within the organization
